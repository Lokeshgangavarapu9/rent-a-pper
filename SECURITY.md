# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of RentForPeer seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please report it to us as soon as possible.

**Please DO NOT open a public GitHub issue.**

Instead, please send an email to [lokeshgangavarapu1@gmail.com](mailto:lokeshgangavarapu1@gmail.com) with the subject line "Security Vulnerability Report - RentForPeer".

In your report, please include:

- A clear and concise description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
- The potential impact of the vulnerability.
- Any suggested mitigations or fixes (if you have them).

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and aim to provide a more detailed response within 5 business days. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities.

## Disclosure Policy

- We will keep you informed of the progress towards a fix.
- We will not disclose the vulnerability publicly until a fix is available and deployed.
- We will credit you for your discovery if you wish to be publicly acknowledged.

Thank you for helping to keep RentForPeer secure!
